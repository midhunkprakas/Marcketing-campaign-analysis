## 📊 Customer Personality Analysis

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a customer marketing dataset to understand customer demographics, spending patterns, and purchasing behavior.

### 🔍 Key Objectives
- Analyze income distribution and customer demographics
- Understand customer spending behavior and high-value segments
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Evaluate marketing campaign effectiveness
- Explore relationships between income, spending, and other variables

### 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly)
- Jupyter Notebook

### 📈 Key Insights
- Majority of customers belong to the mid-income segment
- A small group of high-value customers contributes significantly to revenue
- Strong positive relationship between income and spending
- Wines and meat products are the top revenue-generating categories
- Campaign response rate is relatively low, indicating scope for improvement

### 🚀 Outcome
The analysis provides actionable insights to improve marketing strategies, target high-value customers, and optimize product offerings.
